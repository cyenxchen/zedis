# Privacy Policy for Zedis

**Last updated: December 30, 2025**

## English

Zedis ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our macOS application "Zedis" handles your information.

**1. Data Collection**
Zedis does **not** collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information, usage data, or analytics.

**2. Data Usage**
The application functions solely as a client to connect to your Redis servers. All data processing (viewing keys, executing commands) occurs locally on your device or directly between your device and your Redis server. We do not have access to your Redis data.

**3. Third-Party Services**
Zedis does not use any third-party analytics SDKs or tracking tools.

**4. Contact Us**
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub Issues page: https://github.com/vicanso/zedis/issues
